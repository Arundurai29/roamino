About Roamino

At Roamino, we're leading the charge to revolutionize the Travel & Tourism industry by decentralizing it and empowering both travelers and providers. Q. What is an ICO sale?

An Initial Coin Offering (ICO) is a method of fundraising that involves issuing and selling digital tokens in exchange for other cryptocurrencies or fiat currencies.

Q. How do I participate in an ICO sale?

To participate in an ICO sale, you need to have a cryptocurrency wallet like Metamask or Trustwallet that supports the tokens. You then send the required amount of cryptocurrency to the ICO sale address provided by the issuer to buy.

Q. WWhat is a whitepaper and why is it important for an ICO sale?

A whitepaper is a detailed document that explains the project, its goals, and how it plans to achieve them. It also provides information on the team behind the project, the token economics, and the use cases for the token. A whitepaper is important for an ICO sale because it gives investors a better understanding of the project and helps them make informed investment decisions.

Q. How do I store the tokens I receive from an ICO sale?

You can store the tokens you receive from an ICO sale in a cryptocurrency wallet that supports the specific token.

Q. What are the risks involved in participating in an ICO sale?

The risks involved in participating in an ICO sale include price volatility, fraud, or lack of regulation. It's important to conduct thorough research on the project and team behind the ICO before investing.

Q. What is the difference between a utility token and a security token?

A utility token is a token that is designed to be used within a specific ecosystem or platform. A security token, on the other hand, represents ownership in an asset or company and is subject to securities laws and regulations.

Q. Can I participate in an ICO sale from any country?

The legality of ICO sales varies from country to country. Some countries have banned ICO sales altogether, while others have implemented regulations to govern them. It's important to check the laws and regulations in your country before participating in an ICO sale.
