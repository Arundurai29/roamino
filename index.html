About Roamino

Roamino is a Next-gen online travel booking platform driven by the cutting-edge Blockchain technology with the Ultimate goal of travel decentralization. Roamino provides a wide range of Hotels, Flights, and other travel facilities for roamers to book and plan their Holidays securely & instantly.

  • Blockchain Powered
  • Transparent
  • High Standard Security
  • Location NFTs
  • No hefty Commissions
  • Flexible Payment Solutions
  • All-in-one Travel platform
  • Tokenized Rewards

Roamino platformv1

Roamino's love for travel and technology has made it one of the most robust blockchain-based online travel booking platforms that enable users to book hotels, holiday events, flights, and other transport facilities online.

System features

  • A one-stop destination for roamers.

  • Ensure a transparent ecosystem, Blockchain- based review system, less commission, and more secure.

  • An efficient and economical travel booking platform that empowers tokenized incentives.

  • More payment flexibility, which includes all Fiat currencies & Cryptocurrencies

Loaction NFT’s / Geo NFT’s

Bringing the NFT glory onto Roamino’s platform with the top utility. Introducing Roamino’s Location-specific NFT collection.

System features

  • Limited collections of Location-based NFTs based on popular tourist spots.

  • People who hold specific region NFT and travel to the same location can avail of discounts or free travel or stays.

  • Digital collectibles will play an active role in our loyalty program, exclusive club memberships, discounts, and more.

  • Free Metaverse Travel Access

Metaverse travel

Roamino will be adding one of the newest technologies to the platform, Metaverse Travel. Although it’s a long-term community-driven specter, Roamino will be the early one to explore and work on creating a Virtual travel space.

System features

  • Roamino will eliminate the need to travel physically across the world by showcasing the destinations virtually.

  • VR/AR compatibility and internet connectivity are all you need.

  • 3D digital verse of the location will whet a wanderlust’s appetite for real destinations.

  • The metaverse can be used to organize events, concerts, conferences, exhibitions, etc.

DeRoam

DeRoam (Decentralised Roaming) platform is an end-to-end decentralized travel booking platform that connects commuters and property owners directly without any intermediaries.

System features

  • END-TO-END Decentralized Travel booking platform

  • 0% commission

  • Smart Contract Automation, powered by blockchain technology

  • Secured, P2P verifications and a completely user-centric Travel platform
